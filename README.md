This project focuses on analyzing customer behavior and marketing performance to drive data-driven business decisions. The dashboard provides insights into customer demographics, purchasing patterns, and campaign effectiveness, helping organizations optimize their marketing strategies.

The analysis includes key metrics such as customer acquisition, retention rate, customer lifetime value (CLV), sales growth, and campaign ROI. By segmenting customers based on factors like region, purchase frequency, and product preferences, the dashboard enables targeted marketing and personalized engagement.

Interactive visualizations allow users to explore trends over time, compare performance across channels, and identify high-value customer segments. The project also involves data cleaning, transformation, and modeling to ensure reliable and actionable insights.

Key features include:

Customer segmentation and behavior analysis
Marketing campaign performance evaluation
Sales and revenue trend analysis
Customer retention and churn insights
Channel-wise marketing effectiveness
